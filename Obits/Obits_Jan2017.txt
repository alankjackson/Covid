Maria P. Zepeda 1924-2017 It is with sadness that we, the family of Maria Ponce Zepeda, 92, announce that she passed away peacefully in her sleep on Thursday, January 12, 2017. She was born on September 25, 1924 in McFaddin, Texas to Isabel and Tomas Ponce.
